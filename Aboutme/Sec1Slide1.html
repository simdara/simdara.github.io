Company in brief 1/2

  • Established in July 1998 as a state-owned enterprise (SOE), PPAP is the largest river port operator and the second largest international container terminal port in Cambodia.
  • PPAP was awarded the exclusive river port operating license in Jan 2009 to operate within a given service territory that extends from Phnom Penh to: 1) Neak Leurng at Lower-Mekong River (56km); 2) Tonle Bet at Upper-Mekong River (105km) 3) Tonle Sap (7km).
  • PPAP has the authority to regulate and oversee other private terminals (such as petroleum, gas terminals and other cargo terminals) within its commercial zone.
  • In mid 4Q15, PPAP launched an IPO through which it offered 4,136,873 shares or 20% of ownership to the public. Following this IPO, the oport operator was listed on the CSX in Dec 2015.
